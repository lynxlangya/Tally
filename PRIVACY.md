# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Tally - Simple Expense Tracker is a local-first personal finance app developed by Yunfan Wang.

## Data Stored by the App

Tally stores the information you enter in the app on your device, including:

- Bills, income, expenses, categories, dates, and notes
- Recurring transaction settings
- App preferences such as language, theme, and display symbol
- Optional profile information or avatar configured inside the app

Widgets may read summary snapshots from the app's local App Group storage so they can display quick-entry and trend information on your device.

## Data Collection

Tally does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal finance data.

This version does not include:

- User accounts
- Remote sync
- Third-party analytics
- Advertising SDKs
- Cross-app or cross-website tracking

## Import and Export

Tally includes manual import and export features. Exported CSV or backup files are created only when you choose to export them. You are responsible for where you save or share exported files.

## Notifications

If enabled, Tally may use local notifications for reminders. Notification settings are controlled by iOS. Reminder data stays on your device.

## Support

If you contact support, you may choose to provide information such as your question, issue details, screenshots, or contact information. This information is used only to respond to your request and improve the app.

Support page: https://github.com/lynxlangya/Tally/issues

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app's data handling changes. The latest version will be published at this page.
